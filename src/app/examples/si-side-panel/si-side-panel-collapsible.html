Collapsible Side Panel

This example demonstrates a collapsible side panel with mobile support enabled. The panel can be collapsed to a minimal width while maintaining visibility of key navigation elements.

Over: Panel overlays content for temporary interactions. Scroll: Panel pushes content aside for persistent workflows. Regular (390px): Standard width for navigation and settings. Wide (500px): Expanded width for complex forms and content. Extended (Responsive): Adaptive width scaling from mobile full-width to desktop 912px max.
