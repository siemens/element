Modes and sizes

Over: The side panel overlays on top of the main content, which remains in its original position. Ideal for temporary interactions without losing context. Scroll: The side panel pushes the main content to the left, resizing the available space. Best for persistent side-by-side workflows.

Regular (390px): Standard width suitable for most content like navigation, settings, or simple forms. Wide (500px): Expanded width for more complex content, detailed forms, or when more reading space is needed.
